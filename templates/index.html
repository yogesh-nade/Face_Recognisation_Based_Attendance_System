About Us

Welcome to the Face Recognition Attendance System. Our mission is to provide a seamless and efficient way of marking attendance using cutting-edge computer vision and machine learning technologies.

This system automates the attendance process by recognizing faces, eliminating the need for manual entries or biometric devices. It ensures accuracy, security, and real-time tracking of attendance records.

Key Features:

  • Quick and accurate face recognition for attendance marking.
  • Secure student data storage and management.
  • Detailed analytics and visual reports on attendance trends.
  • Easy-to-use interface for adding students and managing records.

Designed for schools, colleges, and workplaces, our system simplifies the attendance process while reducing fraud and errors.
